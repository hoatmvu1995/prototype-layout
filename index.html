Welcome to C.R.A.W.L.!

Configured Real-Time Application Web Lurker. The purpose of this web application is to help Sac State users find information that's relevant to their needs on Reddit. This will help users who want to research more information from Reddit without having to through millions of posts is what “C.R.A.W.L” can do best.

CRAWL

What is Crawl?

  • A forum spider that navigates through all CSUS' Reddit to deliver student, faculty, parents, and others interested in the current events of California State University, Sacramento (CSUS).
  • Registered users are expected to be affiliated with the CSUS as either student, professor, or faculty member.
  • Registered users can save their searches so that they won't forget on what they've searched for previously.

Why use crawl?

  • Different levels of CRAWL depending on account type of user. (ie. regular, administrator, and guest).
  • Advanced search functions are provided to all users to simplify their searches or make their searches more precisse.
  • Examples of Advanced search functions: From and to dates, search topics, subject keywords to simplify the general searches.
  • Registed users (Regular and Administrator users only) have save history to conveniently search for the same words and/or phrases again.
